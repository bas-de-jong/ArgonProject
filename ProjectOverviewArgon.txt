Plan van aanpak

Begin met maken van programma voor N deeltjes, die een LJ interactie hebben en een periodic BC hebben.

	Initialiseren:
	
		Initialise variable for R and P, which include the position and momenta of all particles.
		
		Set position according to bravais-fcc lattice
		Set momenta according to Maxwell-Boltzmann and normalize drift to zero and scale v to assume T.
		Initialize forces

	Computation:
	
		Compute the forces. Take care of the BC.
		Use Verlets algorithm to compute R,P and F at t+h:
			Compute R, use periodic BC
			Check for exits -> place back in volume
			Compute F at t+h
			Compute P at t+h			
		Repeat for many timesteps
		Every n timesteps rescale v to keep T, (also rescale v to adjust for drift velocity?)
	
	Run Checks:
		Is E conserved? Is P conserved?
